All effects are support display in specific shape area.
  => Circle shape area. The shape can follow the cursor position of your mouse.

There are two demo scene "NPR/NPR.unity" and "Mosaic/Mosaic.unity" demonstrates all the usage of effects.
Just open demo scene, click play button and play with these effects.
Change effect by select "Main Camera" game object, tweak effect paramters in inspector.
Please refer to it as example usage.

Although shaders are optimized, camera effect is relative heavily on mobile platform.
Please use it carefully.
